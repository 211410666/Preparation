Indicative list of materials or parts to be identified for selective treatment(排外部位)

- Parts containing polychlorinated biphenyls (PCB) 含有多氯聯苯的部位

- Btteries 電池

- Printed circuit boards if larger than 10 cm^2 大於100平方公分的電路板

- Toner cartridges, liquid and pasty, as well as color toner 碳粉匣

- Plastic containing regulated flame retardants 含有阻燃劑之塑膠

- Parts containing asbestos 含有石綿之零件

- Cathode ray tubes (CRTs) 陰極射線管

- Chlorofluorocarbons (CFC), hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFC) or hydrofluorocarbons (HFC), fluid hydrocarbons (HC)

- Gas discharge lamps 氣體放電燈

- Liquid crystal displays (together with their casing where appropriate) of a surface greater than 100 cm2 and all those back-lighted with gas discharge lamps 大於100平方公分的液晶螢幕(且連同外殼)

- External electric cables 外部CABLE

- Components containing refractory ceramic fibres 含有耐火陶瓷的零件

- Components containing radioactive substances 含有放射性物質的零件

- Electrolyte capacitors containing substances of concern (height > 25 mm, diameter > 25 mm or proportionately similar volume)
